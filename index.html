This was GOODBIDS.

GOODBIDS was a novel approach to online fundraising - positive auctions, with a twist. Every bid was a donation, and every donation increased in value.

In the auctions we ran, nonprofits like Save The Children and Charity:Water raised thousands of dollars with online auctions that took minutes to set up. Supporters won prizes like Apple Watches, appearances in Future Best-Selling novels, and concert tickets, all for a fraction of their market value.

While the original intent was to commercialize the idea, we've now pivoted to open-sourcing the code with the hope that someone will bring this idea to the world. The source code is available here.

We reserve the right to our domain, to the name Goodbids and to any other trademarks or partnerships, but you're welcome to fork the code and build your own platform.
